# Incident Response

Purpose
- Define incident lifecycle, roles, and communication channels.
- Scope: incident detection, triage, mitigation, escalation, and postmortem.

Roles
- Incident Commander (IC)
- Communications lead
- SRE/On-call responder
- Engineering owner

Process
1. Detect and acknowledge incident.
2. Triage severity and assign IC.
3. Apply mitigation steps from relevant runbook.
4. Communicate status to stakeholders.
5. Post-incident review and actions.

Communication
- Use status page and incident channel for external/internal updates.
